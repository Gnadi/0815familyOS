2. Data We Collect

  • Account data: email address, display name, authentication provider (Email/Password, Google, or Apple), and a unique user ID issued by Firebase Authentication.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide the core features of Family OS (sign-in, sync between family members, reminders).
  • To secure your account against unauthorized access.

4. Legal Basis (GDPR)

We process your personal data based on (a) performance of the contract you enter into when you create an account (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR), and (b) our legitimate interest in operating a secure service (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR).

5. Sharing With Third Parties

  • Google Firebase (Google Ireland Ltd.) — authentication and database hosting. Data is stored on Google Cloud servers in the EU region. See Firebase Privacy.
  • Apple — only when you use "Sign in with Apple". Apple may receive your email (optionally hidden via Apple Relay) and name during initial sign-up.

We do not sell your personal data. We do not use third-party advertising or tracking SDKs.

6. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account exists. You can delete your account at any time from the Settings screen; all family content owned only by you will be removed within 30 days.

7. Your Rights

Under the GDPR you have the right to access, rectify, delete, restrict, or port your personal data, and to object to its processing. Send requests to TODO@example.com. You may also lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

8. Children

Family OS is intended for adults (parents or guardians). Children's data is only entered by their parent or guardian under their responsibility. We do not knowingly collect data directly from children under 13.

9. Security

Data in transit is encrypted via TLS. Family content is additionally encrypted at the family level with keys generated on first family creation. Authentication is handled by Firebase Auth.
